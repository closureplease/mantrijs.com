MantriDependency System

Why a new system?

A dependency system helps you develop, manage and maintain web applications. A module loader takes care of asynchronously loading components that your app requires. Using a module loader as a dependency system is an overkill. It is time to decouple module loading from dependency management.

Why use Mantri?

Harder, Better, Faster, Stronger. Mantri leaves no footprint on your deployed file, which means faster delivery of your app. Provides dead easy testing by exposing all your methods and properties on development, hides them on production. Simple and lean provide and require statements will make your day! And finally a complete workflow to build your codebase.

How Mantri works
